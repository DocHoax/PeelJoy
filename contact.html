Frequently Asked Questions

Are all the design assets really free?

Yes! All icons, illustrations, 3D assets, and Lottie animations on PeelJoy are completely free to download and use in your personal and commercial projects.

Attribution is appreciated but not required. Feel free to use our assets in your projects without any credit obligations.

Absolutely! Our assets can be used for both personal and commercial projects. Please refer to our Terms of Service for more details.
